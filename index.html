Research Work

This section describes my contributions within collaborative Penn State research projects. The independent projects in the next section are separate and are the primary code samples highlighted on GitHub.

TWIRLeT-2.5D — First-Author Research

Reinforcement Learning for 2.5D Chiplet Placement

Developed and evaluated a reinforcement-learning workflow for thermally aware 2.5D chiplet placement using imitation learning, PPO, placement constraints, MILP-based routing, and thermal simulation.

My contributions included experiment design, model and environment development, automated evaluation, benchmarking, failure analysis, and comparison against simulated-annealing and optimization-based baselines. The reported system reduced wirelength by up to 27% and achieved 58× faster time to solution than simulated annealing.

Reinforcement Learning · Imitation Learning · PPO · Python · PyTorch · Thermal Simulation · MILP · Optimization

Oscillator-Based Ising and GNN Freeze-Mask Research

Combinatorial Optimization and Graph Learning

Contribute to oscillator-based Ising-machine workflows for Max-Cut-style combinatorial problems and graph-neural-network methods that identify stable node decisions for selective freezing.

My work includes graph-level and node-level data collection, C++/MPI/OpenMP experimentation, runtime and solution-quality analysis, freeze-mask evaluation, and generalization studies on unseen graph instances.

C++ · MPI · OpenMP · PyTorch · GNNs · Ising Models · Max-Cut · Benchmarking
